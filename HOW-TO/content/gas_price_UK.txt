The function categorises outputs into three distinct ranges: low (1-30 Gwei), moderate (30-200 Gwei), and peak (200-5000 Gwei). This structured approach ensures that the generated gas_price values are representative of real-world scenarios, from quiet periods of low activity to high-intensity token launches or market liquidations. By utilising these tiers, developers can stress-test their smart contract logic and front-end estimations against extreme spikes in transaction costs without the need for an active testnet connection.</p>
